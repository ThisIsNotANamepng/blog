# Applied Influence Warfare

This is an academic style paper designed to introduce a field of security I came up myself. This is not the work of a security professional. These are the researched musings of an security student. This is also a living document, things might change as I do more research and change my mind. 

In my [introductory article to Influence Warfare](/research/Introduction To Influence Warfare) I introduced my definition of Influence Warfare, I suggest you read it first in order to understand this article. 

These are examples of different examples of IW, later in the article I will classify them as branches within IW.

## Large Scale Disinformation Campaigns

Examples of an organization spewing disinformation in order to sway public opinion

## Targeted Disinformation Campaigns 

Examples of an organization spewing disinformation to a specific audience in order to sway their opinion or strike a chord within them

## Whaling Campaigns

Examples of showing disinformation to a single or multiple people in order to change their opinion (Israel intelligence targeting US lawmakers online)

## Population Control

Examples of restricting access to certain information or media to a local populace (China, Russia)

## Health Operations

Examples of operations specifically designed to demoralize a populace by sowing conflicting or demoralizing information. Specifically designed to affect the mental and eventual physical health of a populace
