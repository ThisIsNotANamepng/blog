What I'm Doing Now

Updated January 1st, 2025

Winter break

I'm out for winter break until late January, so I've been using my newfound free time to work on some personal research and projects, largely building out ideas I want to pursue with the cybersecurity club that I formed. (The Blugold Group).

I've ported some major features to our internal ctf website, refactored our TALONS (our custom built toolkit for CTFs), and completely rethought how we build Sicarius, our custom LLM which specialized in cybersecurity, specifically for helping our members during cyber engagements. So far I've built out a LLM fine tuning pipeline and created two datasets completely from the ground up for fine tuning. I am so lucky that my university has an HPC center.

As of right now I'm the only one working on these projects (besides the website, I have one member who's picked up some feature requests there), but I plan to change that with this upcoming semester as we get established and I can train our members. For all of the clubs I run I'm planning a "spring blitz", big campaigns to improve member engagement and generally make the clubs better. I founded the Model UN and Cybersecurity clubs last semester, but I also became the president of my university's student chapter of the ACM for this upcoming semester.
